The pubclub

The pubclub is a thing where sometimes someone does something. But, most importantly, the pubclub is devoted to visiting pubs, with the ephemeral objective of getting a PubHD.

This objective has been achieved on 4 April 2007, exactly three years after the beginning of the pubclub project. After that, the pubclubbers worked hard on their PubDoc, before concluding this experience on 19 June 2008.
